Can I appeal an international arbitral award?

   No, an international arbitration award rendered in France can only be
   challenged for annulment and cannot be appealed.  The action for
   annulment is introduced within a month (from the notification of the
   award).

Can I appeal a national arbitral award?

   You can not appeal, unless both parties agree to.

   This is one of the advantages of arbitration: it enables the parties to
   obtain a final decision in a few months (an average of 8 to 12 months
   with the CMAP). Whereas several years would be necessary if the case
   was taken before national judges.

Does turning to alternative dispute resolution methods jeopardizes my chances
to go to court?

   No, either party is free to put an end to the process at any time.
